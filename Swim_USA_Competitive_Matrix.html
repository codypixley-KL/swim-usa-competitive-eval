Competitive Questions
Braze excels for app-first, non-commerce brands where mobile push and in-app messaging are the main levers. For ecommerce brands whose growth engine is email and SMS on Shopify, Braze's event-driven architecture and MAU-based pricing often mean more data mapping, more engineering ownership, and less predictability. Klaviyo is a B2C ecommerce CRM built for Shopify-first brands — unifying data, analytics, and activation so marketers can own personalization at scale without a heavy CDP or dev layer.
